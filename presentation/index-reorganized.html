Source: SingStat
Median monthly income, income by deciles, changes over time

Household Expenditure Survey

Source: Singapore Government
Spending patterns across housing, food, transport categories
Features: Spending volatility, discretionary vs non-discretionary ratios

National Accounts & GDP

Source: Data.gov.sg
GDP, private consumption, government expenditure, investment data
Features: Macro-level benchmarking, trend normalization

Government Finance & Fiscal

Source: Ministry of Finance

Features: Policy impact correlation, external risk prediction

Project Scope

In Scope

  • Official Singapore datasets (Data.gov.sg, SingStat)
  • Derived features (savings rate, spending ratios)
  • Synthetic unstructured data (sentiment, tone)
  • AI/ML modeling (prediction, segmentation)
  • Voice-enabled financial profiling
  • Real-time scenario modeling
  • Personalized recommendations

Out of Scope

  • Cross-border financial datasets
  • Real-time market feeds
  • Real client data (anonymized/synthetic only)
  • Certified financial advice
  • Investment execution
  • Regulatory compliance automation

Implementation Phases

1

Customer Fact Find

Data Collection

  • Get comprehensive data from customer
  • Create detailed customer profile
  • Identify missing datasets for follow-up
  • Focus on income, expenses, assets, liabilities

2

Life Stage Analysis

Predictive Modeling

  • Plot active income, passive income, savings projections
  • AI models predict expense/income changes annually
  • Segment customer based on current life stage
  • Account for expected life changes (marriage, children)
Output: 30-year financial trajectory with life stage adjustments
